About me

 

Who I am
I'm a postgraduate student and applying for PhD program, with great enthusiasm for chemical scientific research to pursue research on explicit directions of Chemistry, Metallic Catalysis and Nano-materials Application. My primary major is Environmental Science, which as an interdisciplinaries, thus have many directions like sustainable enenrgy production,greenable chemistry, and lot of others direction could be further studied. During my Master’s degree in Shanghai Jiaotong University, I did some academic researches in noble-bimetallic catalysis for nitrates reduction. Most importantly, I aim to continue pursuing my research in Chemistry direction, broaden my insights with more professional knowledge, be commited to explore intriguing catalytic world. I also have many others interests like Photography, video clip, traveling and music. For me, the meaning of life is to be a much more professional and wonderful person.
